Contact Form Scripts

Contact form customization

There are 2 types of contact forms, both are working and valid.

Minimal contact form

The simple contact with name, email or message input fields

Folder - contact_form_2

Required files

<script src="contact_form_2/contact.js"></script>

Open the file for add your email: contact_form_2 / process-contact.php

Replace your email with given address below
$my_email = 'support@example.com'; // Change with your email address

Contact form with captcha

The simple contact with name, email or message input fields

Folder - form

Required files

<script src="js/contact-form.js"></script>

Open the file for add your email: form / library/ sendmail.php

Replace your email with given address below
define("MY_EMAIL", "yourmail@domain.com");
